Grumpy Cat

Tardar Sauce, known as Grumpy Cat, became an internet sensation in 2012 due to her permanently grumpy facial expression caused by feline dwarfism.

Her unique look made her one of the most recognizable cats in the world.

Nala Cat

Nala holds a Guinness World Record for having the most followers on Instagram for a cat.

This cross-eyed Siamese-Tabby mix was adopted from a shelter and now uses her fame to promote animal adoption worldwide.

Maru

Maru is a Scottish Fold from Japan who has captivated millions with his love of boxes and playful personality.

His YouTube channel has accumulated hundreds of millions of views, making him one of the most watched cats online.

Lil Bub

Lil Bub was known for her unique appearance resulting from genetic mutations, including dwarfism and an extra toe on each paw.

She became a symbol of perseverance and was used to raise awareness for special needs pets everywhere.

Colonel Meow

Colonel Meow held the Guinness World Record for having the longest fur on a cat, measuring nine inches.

This Himalayan-Persian crossbreed had a grumpy demeanor that endeared him to fans across social media platforms.

Keyboard Cat

Keyboard Cat became one of the earliest viral cat videos on the internet. The original video from 1984 showed a cat appearing to play an electronic keyboard.

It has been used in countless remixes and internet memes since.
